# FinOpsIQ — Sales Overview

## Headline
**Cut Your Cloud Bill by 40% with AI-Powered Cost Intelligence**

## The Problem
Engineering teams waste $15M+ per year on average in unused cloud resources. Idle EC2 instances, over-provisioned databases, forgotten storage buckets — the waste accumulates silently while finance teams scramble to understand skyrocketing cloud bills.

## The Solution
FinOpsIQ connects to all your cloud accounts, ingests cost data in real-time, and uses GPT-4o to identify specific waste opportunities with actionable recommendations. Set budgets, catch anomalies before they become problems, and give every team visibility into their cloud spend.

## Key Features
- **Real-Time Cost Visibility** — See exactly where every dollar is going across AWS, GCP, and Azure
- **AI Waste Detection** — Identifies idle resources, oversized instances, and unused reservations automatically
- **Budget Enforcement** — Set team/project budgets with auto-alerts and enforcement actions
- **Anomaly Alerts** — Catch spend spikes the moment they happen with AI-generated explanations
- **Cost Attribution** — Allocate costs to teams, projects, and environments using tag-based rules
- **Executive Reports** — One-click PDF/CSV exports for finance reviews
