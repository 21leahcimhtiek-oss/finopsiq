# FinOpsIQ Product Description

## Tagline

Stop Overpaying for Cloud. Start Saving Today.

## One-Liner

FinOpsIQ is the AI-powered cloud cost management platform that helps engineering teams and finance ops reduce cloud spend by 30% in 90 days.

## Short Description (< 150 words)

FinOpsIQ connects your AWS, GCP, and Azure accounts in minutes and immediately starts finding waste. Our AI engine analyzes your cost patterns, detects anomalies before they become budget disasters, and generates actionable savings recommendations personalized to your infrastructure.

Unlike generic cost dashboards, FinOpsIQ uses ML-powered anomaly detection with GPT-4 explanations, so your team knows not just WHAT is expensive, but WHY and HOW to fix it. Set budgets with smart alerts, track savings progress, and generate chargeback reports for internal billing.

Trusted by startups to Fortune 500s. No agents to install. No complex setup. Just connect your billing CSV and start saving.

## Key Features

- Multi-cloud support: AWS, GCP, Azure
- AI anomaly detection with natural language explanations
- ML cost forecasting (90-day outlook)
- Actionable savings recommendations
- Budget management with threshold alerts
- Team collaboration with role-based access
- Stripe-powered subscription billing
- SOC2-ready architecture with AES-256-GCM encryption
